FAQ

Got Questions? We've Got Answers

Everything you need to know about the ZynkLoot 7-digit draw.

Each draw generates a random 7-digit number (0000000–9999999). You pick your own 7-digit number and buy a ticket. If your number matches the drawn number exactly, you win the grand prize of 7 Crore Zynk. Three separate draws happen every day.
Instead of revealing all 7 digits at once, ZynkLoot reveals one digit per hour over a 7-hour window. This creates a live, suspenseful experience and lets you decide when to buy your ticket based on how many digits have already been shown.
The earlier you buy your ticket, the higher your multiplier. Before any digits are revealed, you get a 49x multiplier at just 100 Zynk. After 1 digit is revealed, the multiplier drops to 36x and the price rises to 136 Zynk. This continues until all 7 digits are shown, when the multiplier is 1x and the ticket costs 4,900 Zynk. The multiplier applies to your winnings.
Yes. You can buy a ticket at any point during the draw — even after all 7 digits are revealed. The trade-off is that later tickets cost more and carry a lower multiplier. Buying early is riskier but far more rewarding; buying late gives you more information but at a premium price.
If your chosen number matches the drawn number exactly, you win the grand prize of 7 Crore Zynk. Your winnings are multiplied by the tier at which you purchased your ticket. For example, if you bought at the 49x tier (100 Zynk), your effective payout reflects that maximum multiplier.
There are 3 draws every day — morning, afternoon, and evening. Each draw is independent with its own 7-digit number and 7-hour reveal window. You can participate in one, two, or all three draws each day.
Go to your dashboard, tap “Withdraw,” choose your preferred method (bank transfer or e-wallet), enter the amount, and confirm. Payouts are processed instantly. There are no withdrawal fees — you keep every Zynk you win.
